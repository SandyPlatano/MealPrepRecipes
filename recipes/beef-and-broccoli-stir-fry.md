# Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

## Protein Type
Beef

## Prep Time
15 minutes

## Cook Time
15 minutes

## Servings
4

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs flank steak, sliced thin
- 4 cups broccoli florets
- 1/3 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup beef broth
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Instructions
1. Mix soy sauce, broth, brown sugar, and cornstarch
2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat
3. Add beef and stir-fry until browned
4. Remove beef and set aside
5. Add sesame oil, garlic, and ginger to wok
6. Cook 30 seconds
7. Add broccoli and stir-fry 3-4 minutes
8. Return beef to wok
9. Pour sauce over and cook until thickened
10. Serve over rice
