# Honey Garlic Pork Chops

## Protein Type
Pork

## Prep Time
10 minutes

## Cook Time
20 minutes

## Servings
4

## Ingredients
- 4 bone-in pork chops
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
3. Sear pork chops 4-5 minutes per side
4. Remove pork chops and set aside
5. In same skillet, add garlic and cook 30 seconds
6. Add honey, soy sauce, vinegar, and red pepper flakes
7. Simmer until sauce thickens slightly
8. Return pork chops to skillet and coat with sauce
9. Cook 2-3 more minutes until done
10. Serve with vegetables and rice
