# Sheet Pan Lemon Herb Chicken

## Protein Type
Chicken

## Prep Time
15 minutes

## Cook Time
35 minutes

## Servings
4

## Ingredients
- 8 pieces chicken thighs
- 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
- 1 lb green beans
- 2 lemons
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp fresh rosemary
- 2 tbsp fresh thyme
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper
3. Toss chicken and potatoes with marinade
4. Arrange on a sheet pan
5. Roast for 25 minutes
6. Add green beans to pan
7. Roast another 10 minutes until chicken is cooked through
8. Serve with lemon wedges
