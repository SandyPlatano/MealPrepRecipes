## Chicken

### Sheet Pan Lemon Herb Chicken
**Tags:**
- One-pan
- Healthy
- Meal-prep-friendly
- Chicken

**Ingredients:**
- 8 pieces chicken thighs
- 1 lb halved baby potatoes
- 1 lb green beans
- 2 lemon
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp fresh rosemary
- 2 tbsp fresh thyme
- to taste salt
- to taste pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper
3. Toss chicken and potatoes with marinade
4. Arrange on a sheet pan
5. Roast for 25 minutes
6. Add green beans to pan
7. Roast another 10 minutes until chicken is cooked through
8. Serve with lemon wedges

### Thai Basil Chicken Stir-Fry
**Tags:**
- Quick
- Asian
- Chicken

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 lbs sliced chicken breast
- 1 cup leaves thai basil
- 2 sliced bell peppers
- 1 sliced onion
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 2 sliced thai chilies
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat
2. Add chicken and cook until browned
3. Add garlic and chilies, stir-fry 30 seconds
4. Add bell peppers and onion
5. Stir-fry 2-3 minutes
6. Mix soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar
7. Pour sauce over chicken and vegetables
8. Add basil leaves and toss until wilted
9. Serve immediately with rice

### Balsamic Glazed Chicken
**Tags:**
- Healthy
- Quick
- Chicken
- Meal-prep-friendly

**Ingredients:**
- 4 (6 oz each) chicken breast
- 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp honey
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tbsp dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- to taste salt
- to taste pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Season chicken with salt and pepper
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet

4. Remove chicken and set aside
5. In same skillet, add garlic and cook 30 seconds
6. Add balsamic vinegar, honey, mustard, and rosemary
7. Bring to a boil and simmer 5 minutes until thickened
8. Return chicken to skillet
9. Spoon glaze over chicken and cook 2 more minutes
10. Serve with roasted vegetables

### One-Pot Chicken and Rice
**Tags:**
- One-pot
- Easy-cleanup
- Chicken
- Comfort-food

**Ingredients:**
- 6 pieces chicken thighs
- 1.5 cups uncooked rice
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 1 diced onion
- 2 diced carrots
- 1 cup peas
- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- to taste salt
- to taste pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Season chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven
3. Brown chicken on both sides
4. Remove chicken and set aside
5. In same pot, sauté onion and carrots until softened
6. Add garlic and cook 1 minute
7. Add rice and stir to coat
8. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil
9. Place chicken on top of rice
10. Cover and simmer 25-30 minutes
11. Add peas in last 5 minutes
12. Let rest 5 minutes before serving

### Slow Cooker Beef Stew
**Tags:**
- Comfort-food
- Slow-cooker
- Meal-prep-friendly
- Beef

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs cubed beef chuck
- 4 large chopped carrots
- 4 large cubed potatoes
- 1 large diced onion
- 3 stalks chopped celery
- 4 cups beef broth
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 tbsp flour

**Instructions:**
1. Coat beef in flour, salt, and pepper
2. Brown beef in a skillet (optional but recommended)
3. Add beef to slow cooker
4. Add all vegetables
5. Mix broth, tomato paste, garlic, and herbs
6. Pour over beef and vegetables
7. Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours
8. Remove bay leaves before serving

### Mongolian Beef
**Tags:**
- Asian
- Quick
- Beef

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 lbs sliced thin flank steak
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tbsp minced ginger
- 4 chopped green onions
- 1/4 cup cornstarch
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/2 cup water

**Instructions:**
1. Coat beef strips in cornstarch
2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat
3. Fry beef in batches until crispy
4. Remove beef and drain on paper towels
5. Drain most of the oil, leaving 1 tablespoon
6. Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds
7. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and red pepper flakes
8. Bring to a boil and simmer until thickened
9. Add beef back to pan and toss with sauce
10. Garnish with green onions and serve over rice

- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 5 cloves minced garlic
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- to taste salt
- to taste pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat

7. Simmer until sauce thickens slightly
8. Return pork chops to skillet and coat with sauce
9. Cook 2-3 more minutes until done
10. Serve with vegetables and rice

### Vegetarian Stuffed Bell Peppers
**Tags:**
- Vegetarian
- Healthy
- Meal-prep-friendly

**Ingredients:**
- 6 large bell peppers
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 can black beans
- 1 cup corn
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 1 diced onion
- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Cut tops off peppers and remove seeds
3. Sauté onion and garlic until soft
4. Mix quinoa, beans, corn, tomatoes, and spices
5. Stir in half the cheese
6. Stuff peppers with mixture
7. Place in baking dish with 1/2 cup water
8. Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes
9. Remove foil, top with remaining cheese
10. Bake 10 more minutes

- Comfort-food
- Italian

- 1 lb italian sausage
- 4 cups marinara sauce
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tbsp italian seasoning

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)

4. Add marinara sauce to sausage

6. Combine pasta with meat sauce
7. Layer half the pasta in a 9x13 baking dish
8. Spread ricotta mixture over pasta
9. Add remaining pasta on top
10. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan
11. Bake 25-30 minutes until bubbly

### Turkey Meatballs in Marinara
**Tags:**
- Healthy
- Meal-prep-friendly
- Italian

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs ground turkey
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
- 2 egg
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- 4 cups marinara sauce
- 2 tsp italian seasoning
- 1 tsp onion powder
- to taste salt
- to taste pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
2. Mix turkey, breadcrumbs, parmesan, eggs, garlic, parsley, and seasonings
3. Form into 1.5-inch meatballs
4. Place on a baking sheet
5. Bake 20 minutes until cooked through
6. Heat marinara sauce in a large pot
7. Add meatballs to sauce
8. Simmer 10 minutes
9. Serve over pasta or with crusty bread
